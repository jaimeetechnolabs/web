About Jaimee Technolabs

Jaimee Technolabs is a product-focused development studio. We help startups and enterprises design, build and scale mobile & web applications. Our team is experienced across industries — social networking, media & streaming, e-commerce, agri-tech and health & fitness.

We emphasize maintainability, testability and user experience, delivering production-ready apps with analytics, CI/CD, and cloud infrastructure.

Our process: Discovery → UI/UX → Development → QA → Launch → Support
Tech stack: Flutter, Kotlin, Swift, Java, Firebase, Node.js, PostgreSQL, AWS/GCP
